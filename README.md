🚀 SkillChain

Own Your Skills. Verify Instantly. Get Hired Faster.

SkillChain is a blockchain-powered Skill Passport platform that enables students and professionals to securely showcase, verify, and share their skills, certificates, and achievements. By combining Web3 technology with credential verification, SkillChain helps recruiters instantly verify candidate credentials and build trust in the hiring process.

Team: ByteForce

🌟 Problem

Traditional resumes and certificates are difficult to verify, leading to:

- Fake or unverifiable credentials
- Time-consuming verification processes
- Reduced trust between candidates and recruiters

💡 Solution

SkillChain provides a verifiable digital Skill Passport where users can:

- Add and manage skills
- Upload certificates
- Generate NFT-based credentials
- Verify credentials through QR codes
- Share a public professional profile
- Build a reputation score
- Connect their Web3 wallet
